Our Mission

Understanding the relation between the park capacity and the other metric. Creating shiny app that recommends best suitable parking spots with given limits. You can check our app from here.

Brief Information About Dataset

We have two tables in our dataset. First table contains informations about ISPARK park areas. Second table contains hourly percentage of occupancies of these park areas between 19 November 2020 and 2 December 2020. We have collected this data by using IBB OpenData API. You can also access this API from here.

Park Informations Table

Park Informations table contains 9 variables and 577 observations. Descriptions of columns is below.

  • ParkID: Unique identifier of the parking place
  • ParkAdi: Name of the parking place
  • Latitude: Latitude of the parking place
  • Longitude: Longitude of the parking place
  • Kapasitesi: Full capacity of the parking space when it is empty
  • ParkTipi: Types of parking ways of the parking space; Yolst Acik Kapali
  • Ilce: District which parking space in
  • UcretsizParklanmaDk:Free parking duration in minutes
  • AylkAbonelikUcreti:Monthly subscription fee ()

HOURLY OCCUPANCY RATE

The line graph below shows us average hourly occupancy rates of Ispark stations. It can be seen from the graph that average parking occupancy rate during daytime is less than nights. Parking occupancy rate is starting to decrease when working hours started and continue to decrease until approximately 15:00 pm. and it sharply increases by closing hours of working day. The graph shows that Ispark stations mostly used during night.

The line below shows us average hourly parking occupancy Rates by park types of Ispark stations. In all park types, the average occupancy rate decreases during daytime. In other words, in all park types, park stations mostly used during night. Another conclusion we can draw is, in average, it is more probably that chance to find empty parking space in parking garage than open parking lots and parking lots near road.

PRICE HISTOGRAM

The histogram below shows us the count of monthly membership prices for different parking types. It has been seen that monthly price of 400 TL and 100 TL are the least common prices. Count of open parks priced 200 TL and count of parks with 0 price are almost identical to each other. According to the histogram We can say that the count of parks it mostly scattered around 200 TL. Free parking lots which doesnt allow monthly parking are the second most common park type. Also the measured data is a bi-modal distribution with two peaks.

NUMBER OF PARKS ACCORDING TO THEIR TYPE

In this graph if we look at the park type, the district with the highest number is Fatih with the type of YOL ST. For the AIK OTOPARK type again Fatih is the district with the highest number. For the YOL ST type Silivri district has the second highest number. We can say that YOL ST park is the most common type.

FREQUENCY OF THE PARKS

If we look at the frequencies of the parks Fatih district has the highest frequency. After that Beikta and ili districts are almost equal and has the second highest frequency. Arnavutky and Sultangazi are almost equal and has the lowest frequency.
